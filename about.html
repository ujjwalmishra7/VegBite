Our story

Pure vegetarian food, made personal.

Why we started VegBite, what drives us, and the people behind every order.

Since 2021

It started in a home kitchen.

VegBite began with a simple frustration: finding food that was reliably, entirely vegetarian shouldn't require a phone call and a leap of faith. Our founder started cooking thalis for neighbours out of a home kitchen in Indore, and word spread fast.

Today VegBite partners with trusted pure-veg kitchens across the city, each one vetted for the same standard we started with - honest ingredients, no shortcuts, and food that tastes like it was made for you specifically, because it was.

What drives us

Our mission

Food you can trust

Every partner kitchen is 100% vegetarian - no shared equipment, no ambiguity.

Support local kitchens

We work with home cooks and small restaurants, not faceless commercial kitchens.

Reduce food waste

Dishes are cooked closer to order time, so less is thrown away at the end of the day.
